### The begining

It was the year 2011. I was close to completing 1 year in Bangalore. This was the first time I was going to visit Brigade road. I found something very intersting drawn on the wall, and I couldn't take my eyes off of it. So when I visited the same road again, now equipped with a good camera phone, the first thing I did was to capture it and the second thing - geo tag it.

I started noticing the same mural but in different sizes across various locations within Banglaore, and then, in Delhi, and then, in Kolkata and then in Mumbai.

That is where I knew, this seemingly simple piece of art has a very strong story behind it. So I found myself on a quest to passively start searching for the origin story.
